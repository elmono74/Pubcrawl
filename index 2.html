Good To Know

Before You Board

01

Trains run hourly

Saturday BNSF trains keep to the hour, so plan to be back on the platform a few minutes before each departure.

02

Water taxi is seasonal

The Chinatown route runs through summer. Exact boarding time gets confirmed the week of the event.

03

Dress for the whole day

Comfortable shoes, layers for the boat, and something you don't mind wearing from noon to midnight.
